Udon

Description

Ingredients

  • All purpose flour
  • Kosher salt
  • Water

Directions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine flour and salt.
  2. Add 1 1/4 cups water
  3. Use hands to mix until dough starts to come together in a few large lumps.
  4. Firmly press and knead the dough, incorporating any loose flour until there is none left.
  5. If necessary, add a little more water, 1 tablespoon at a time, until you can incorporate all of the flour.
  6. Knead the dough until smooth, about 5 minutes. Cover dough with plastic and let it rest for about an hour. This will make the dough relax and easier to roll out. *Jaden’s note – I like resting the dough for over an hour (timing is flexible here…I’ve let my dough rest anywhere between 1-3 hours, just keep it covered in plastic wrap), it just makes it so much easier to roll out!
